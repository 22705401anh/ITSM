## ?? 14 Columns

1. ? **Full Name** - Employee name
2. ? **User ID** - Employee/Badge ID
3. ? **Department** - Department name
4. ? **Host Name** - Computer name
5. ? **Assigned** - Yes/No status (color-coded)
6. ? **Laptop Model** - Laptop model
7. ? **Laptop SN** - Laptop serial number
8. ? **Monitor Model** - Monitor model
9. ? **Monitor SN** - Monitor serial number
10. ? **Docking SN** - Docking station SN
11. ? **Accessories** - Keyboard, mouse, etc.
12. ? **Phone Model** - Mobile phone model
13. ? **Phone #** - Phone number/extension
14. ? **Actions** - View, Edit, Delete buttons
